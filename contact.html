Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days within the US. International orders typically arrive in 7-14 business days.

What's your return policy?

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. If you're not satisfied, return the product for a full refund or exchange.

Are your products cruelty-free?

Yes! All GlowBeauty products are 100% cruelty-free and never tested on animals. Many of our products are also vegan.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes, we offer free standard shipping on all orders over $50 within the United States.

Can I track my order?

Absolutely! Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email to monitor your delivery in real-time.

Are ingredients listed on products?

Yes, we believe in full transparency. Every product page lists the complete ingredient list so you know exactly what goes on your skin.
